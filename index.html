Pizza

Pizza is a popular Italian dish consisting of a round dough base, topped with tomato sauce, cheese, and various toppings. It is typically baked in an oven until the crust is crispy and the cheese is melted. Pizza is a widely consumed fast food enjoyed all over the world. .

Sandwich

A sandwich is a type of food consisting of two or more slices of bread, with fillings such as meats, cheese, vegetables, spreads, or sauces. The bread can be toasted, grilled, or left untoasted. Sandwiches are a popular fast food and are often eaten as a meal or a snack. .

Burrito

A burrito is a type of Mexican food consisting of a flour tortilla wrapped around a filling of rice, beans, meat, cheese, and vegetables. It is often served with additional toppings such as salsa, sour cream, or guacamole. Burritos are a popular fast food and are typically eaten as a meal.
